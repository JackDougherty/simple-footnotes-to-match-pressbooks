== Description ==

Create simple, elegant footnotes on your site. Use the `[footnote]` shortcode and the plugin takes care of the rest. Modified by Jack to make shortcode compatible with PressBooks format.

Example usage: `Lorem ipsum. [footnote]My note.[/footnote]`

If you paginate your posts, you can optionally move your footnotes below your page links. Look under Settings > Reading. Footnotes will still appear as normal for posts that are unpaginated.
